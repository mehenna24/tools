Scope:<br>
This Standard covers the standardization of dimensions of welded and seamless wrought steel pipe for high or low temperatures and pressures.
<br>
The word "pipe" is used, as distinguished from "tube", to apply to tubular products of dimensions commonly used for pipeline and piping systems.
<br>
<br>
Note:<br>
(1) STD = standard; XS = extra-strong; and XXS = double extra-strong
